- **Part A** strengthens Layer 1 (the conceptual base) with citable, ingestible anatomy nodes.
- **Parts B–C** are the *prevention* and *prehospital/transport* arms of the case journey — primarily pitch-narrative and roadmap material (Kyro's MVP is the OR moment, not these).
- **Part D** is the one with near-term product relevance beyond the OR: the no-CT diagnostic adjuncts (ONSD, POCUS, NIRS, GFAP) are credible *complements* Kyro could surface or integrate, and the headline numbers are strong pitch evidence.
- **Part E** is the long-tail vision (rehab/caregiver/epilepsy) — the post-hackathon expansion that completes the case arc.
